Privacy Policy – Akironex

Effective Date: 30.03.2026

Akironex only uses the software and hardware functionality integrated into the app to operate as intended.

Important information regarding data collection and usage:

  • No data collection: The app does not collect any personal data from users.
  • No data transmission: No data is sent to third parties
  • Local storage: All data created or stored in the app remains exclusively on the device.
  • Push notifications: If the app uses push notifications, they are solely for app functionality. No advertising IDs or tracking information is collected.
  • No advertising or tracking: The app contains no ads and does not use user tracking.
  • User control: Users have full control over their data at all times, as it is stored exclusively on their device.

Data storage and deletion

  • Data retention: Since all data is stored locally on the user's device, it is retained only as long as the user keeps it. The app itself does not define or enforce any retention period.
  • Data deletion: The app does not provide a built-in feature to delete stored data. However, users can delete all app data at any time through their device settings (e.g., by clearing app storage or uninstalling the app), which will permanently remove all locally stored data.
